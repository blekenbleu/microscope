Practically, focusing a 10X objective by bellows

Increased working distance impacts numerical aperture,
but trials with bellows did not indicate any drastic effects,
with results compromised by difficulty focusing.
Eliminating all optics but objective should increase contrast,
but illumination differences and reduction in magnification confound comparison.

Dovetails

Circular dovetails are often used in microscopes
  • attach heads to arms
  • attach accessories (e.g. vertical illuminators) between arm and head
  • attach nosepiece (objective turret) to arm
  • attach stage to stage focus bracket
  • attach condenser to substage

AO 50.5mm dovetail to M42 adapter arrived from RAF Camera.
This secures camera body and extender tubes to A.O. arm
without head (and tube lens).
Finite objectives can then be used in A.O. arm
with much better positioning and focus control than by bellows.

Dovetail diameters:
  • AO infinity head:  50.5mm
      AO infinity dovetail accessories fit Nikon infinity
  • Nikon infinity head:  51mm
      most Nikon finite heads:  49.5mm
      Nikon Biophot/Metaphot (finite) head:  51mm;
      can use Nikon infinity heads with RMS infinity objectives.
  • Olympus head:  42mm
  • condensers

C-mount

Traditionally associated with 16mm movie (then video) cameras,
C-mount (and too similar CS-mount) are often used for microscope camera adapters
and industrial USB/HDMI digital camera bodies,
for which one common brand is Hayear. While there are C-mount cameras with nominal 1-inch sensors, they must suffer vignetting:
  • CS-mount FFD:  12.526mm
  • metric thread spec for both:  25.4x0.75
  • many different sensor sizes:

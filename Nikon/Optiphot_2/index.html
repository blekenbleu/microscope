100W halogen lamp

Halogen cycle lamps operate best with 10% or so of designed voltage.
100W lamp helps make Optiphot 2 good for photography,
but uncomfortably bright for viewing with 20x or lower power objectives.
A variable ND filter can comfortably reduce illumination intensity.
67mm lens filters rest well on the field lens;  72mm variable ND was on sale.

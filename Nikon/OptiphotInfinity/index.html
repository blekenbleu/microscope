    Infinity-focused Optiphot trinocular heads

    Nikon Optiphot (CF) M Plan & EPI objectives have 210mm tube length.
    "F" trinocular head objective rays focus directly at 210mm;
    "T" and "UW" heads convert rays to near infinity around prisms:

    Nikon's CF PL chimney has an M50x0.75 external thread and positive lens.
    Removing that chimney passes infinity rays...
    Alternatively, removing that negative lens would focus infinity objectives,
    but with a bit under a 0.5x magnification.

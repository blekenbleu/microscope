Parfocal DSLR trinocular port

background

Since CF objective introduction,
many Nikon trinocular heads support video camera attachment without relay lens.
This so-called direct projection wants e.g. CS or C-mount with roughly 16 to 24mm diagonal sensor,
which happens to be nearly APS-C, with advantage to Canon's slightly smaller sensors.

Flange to sensor distance

C-mount has 17.526 flange-to-sensor distance.
Most mirrorless camera bodies have compatible specifications,
.. but SLRs require space for mirrors:

A DSLR attached to a C-mount phototube will not be parfocal with eyepieces.
Refocusing objectives for the DSLR is not only inconvenient;
captured images are liable to more aberrations
from objectives beyond their designed working distances.
Problematic aberrations typically increase with N.A. (numeric aperture).

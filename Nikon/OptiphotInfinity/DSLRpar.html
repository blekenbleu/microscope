Flange to sensor distance

C-mount flange-to-sensor distance is 17.526mm;
many interchangeable lens mirrorless camera bodies have similar specifications,
.. but SLR mirror box requires more space:

A DSLR attached to a C-mount phototube CANNOT be parfocal with eyepieces.
Refocusing objectives for the DSLR is not only inconvenient;
captured images are liable to more aberrations
from objectives outside their designed geometries.
Problematic aberrations typically increase with N.A. (numeric aperture).

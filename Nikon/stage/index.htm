Nikon stage bearings

The Metaphot was delivered with its stage partially disassembled and missing a crossed roller bearing rail.
The good news: that missing rail is seemingly the same as Labophot and Optiphot x-travel stage rails,
and an eBay seller lists those racks with rails. Having a stage with stripped thread in its rack,
one of them provided replacement parts for both stages.

Nikon Microscope Mechanical Stage X-rack for Labophot and Optiphot Series

eBay item number:  281567730299
Condition: Used

Nikon x-travel mechanical stage part that includes a good rack,
good roller bearing rails and good cosmetic verniers.
If you have a stage mechanism that is damaged on a Nikon Labophot, Labophot-2, Optiphot or Optiphot-2
this is an easy fix. Even a novice can replace this unit without much trouble.
The sale is for one each as pictured.

A stage must be held on edge to assemble these slides and bearings.

Optiphot stage X-rails will be reused in Metaphot ITP stage Y-rails:

Metaphot stage assembly

Practicing on the Optiphot stage was helpful,
but assembling either would have been nearly impossible
without a hobby vise to hold the stage on edge, tilted back slightly.
Although an Optiphot X rail is the same length as missing Metaphot Y rail,
the Optiphot rail is thinner. Thankfully, it seems to work alright.
